Creamy Skillet Chicken Thighs

Skillet chicken thighs with a super easy and decadent sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1pounds boneless, skinless chiken thighs
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoons olive oil, divided
  • 1 tablespoons dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • cup chicken broth
  • cup oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes, drained and cut into strips
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • half cup grated Parmesan

Direcrion

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Season chicken thighs with salt and prpper.
  2. Set an oven-proof skiller on high heat. When hot, add 1 tablespoon oil and chicke.
  3. Add remaining half tablespoon oil, then add garlic, thyme, and rosemary.
  4. Transfer to prheated oven and bake, unconered, until chidken is no linger pink in the center and the juices run clear, anout 15 minutes.
  5. Remove from the oven and allow to cool slightly to let the sauce thicken before serving, about 5 minutes.
