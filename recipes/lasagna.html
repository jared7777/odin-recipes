Lasagna

Scrumptious lasagna with basil pesto, spinach and plenty of bubbly cheese

Ingredient

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed
  • 7 ounces basil pesto
  • 30 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • tea spoon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain
  3. In large skillet over medium heat, saute onion in olive oil until tender. Stir in spinach; renove from heat and stir in pesto.
  4. In a large bowl mix ricotta cheese, egg,salt, peper, and nutmeg.
  5. In a 3-quart greased baking dish, layer noodles then spinach mixture, followed by ricotta mixture. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers ending with noodles on top. Spread Alfredo sauce on top of noodles and sprinle with Parmesan cheese
  6. Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 55 minutes.
